Computer-Aided Design

Week 3 | Garment & Sublimation Printing

Following our tasks of 3d printing, we were thr introduced to photoshop and had to create using those skills. Wiht this unit, the first task we had was to create a design using AdopePhotoshop to create a personalized image that would end up on a t-shirt. I found this first tep the most difficult as I was unfamiliar with this application, and also struggled with simply coming up with what design I would be able to make and put on a t-shirt. I then decided to make an original design of my favorite rapper, A$AP Rocky. I didn't use just a single image of him, but rather made an original collage of many different pictures which personalized the design. Once that was finished, I was ready to print, and the rest of the process was really easy. Now that I know how to do everything involved in making a shirt, I definitely will be doing it more! After the t-shirt, we had a second assignment of sublimation printing, which I have never heard of until this class. With this, you are able to make a personalized anything! Some options include basketball hoops, ties, socks, keychains, mugs, and so much more. I decided to make a design for a mug because I didn't have one in my dorm room. After creating the image I wanted on the mug, I then had to downnload a template so that I had the right dimensions when exporting onto the flashdrive. After that I was ready to print. The machine prints out special images on certain paper that bonds to an object when heated. It was super cool and something I have never seen before, so I'm really glad I had the opportunity to use that machine! It's crazy what kinds of things you can make on it.
